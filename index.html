Harry James Potter

The Boy Who Lived

Here's a time line of important events in Harry Potter's life:

  • July 31, 1980 - Harry James Potter is born to Lily Evans Potter and James Potter
  • October 31, 1981 - Voldemort kills Harry's parents and his body is killed by his own Killing Curse which rebounds off of Harry
  • July 31, 1991 - Hagrid tracks down Harry and tells him he's a wizard
  • June, 1992 - Harry, Hermione and Ron defeat Quirrel/Voldemort
  • June, 1993 - Harry defeats the shade of Tom Riddle and saves Ginny in the Chamber of Secrets
  • June 6, 1994 - Harry saved Buckbeak and helps reveal the truth about Sirius Black and Peter Pettigrew
  • August 22, 1994 - Harry attends the Quiddich World Cup with the Weasleys. Death Eaters make an appearance
  • June 24, 1995 - Harry and Cedric are kidnapped. Cedric is murdered and Harry barely escapes after dueling Voldemort
  • August 2, 1995 - Harry and Dudley are attacked by dementors
  • June 18, 1996 - The Battle of the Department of Mysteries
  • June, 1997 - Dumbledore is killed by Severus Snape
  • May 2, 1998 - The Battle of Hogwarts
